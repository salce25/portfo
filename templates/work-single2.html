Old School RuneScape/Final Fantasy

Created an immersive mobile game that combines the exploration and open-world elements of Old School RuneScape with the rich storytelling and strategic combat of the Final Fantasy series. Set in a fantasy realm, players will embark on epic quests, engage in thrilling battles, and navigate a vast world filled with diverse landscapes, dungeons, and hidden treasures. The game features character customization, a deep progression system, and a variety of classes and abilities to choose from. Balancing both solo and cooperative gameplay experiences, the game should offer a compelling narrative with memorable characters, challenging boss battles, and a captivating soundtrack. With its nostalgic charm and modern gameplay mechanics, the goal is to provide players with an immersive mobile gaming experience that captures the essence of both Old School RuneScape and Final Fantasy.
